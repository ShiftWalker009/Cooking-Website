Dessert Recipes

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan. In a large bowl, combine flour, cocoa, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract; beat until smooth. Stir in boiling water (the batter will be thin). Pour into the pan and bake for 30–35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Allow to cool before serving.

Apple Pie

Ingredients:

  • 1 package pie crusts (for top and bottom)
  • 6 cups thinly sliced apples (Granny Smith recommended)
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). In a large bowl, mix apples with sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Place one pie crust in a pie pan and fill with the apple mixture. Dot with small pieces of butter, then cover with the top crust (cut slits for steam). Bake for 45–50 minutes until the crust is golden and the apples are tender. Let cool before serving.
