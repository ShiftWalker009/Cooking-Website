Caesar Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 large head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • ½ cup Caesar dressing
  • ¼ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Optional: anchovy fillets, chopped

Instructions:

In a large bowl, toss the chopped lettuce with croutons and Caesar dressing. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese (and anchovies if using) on top. Serve immediately as a fresh, light meal or side dish.
