Savoury Recipes

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with Parmesan and pepper. Drain the pasta (reserve some pasta water) and mix with the pancetta. Remove from heat, then quickly stir in the egg mixture. Adjust consistency with reserved pasta water if needed. Serve immediately.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander (optional)

Instructions:

Sauté onions in a large pan until soft. Add garlic and ginger; cook for another minute. Stir in curry powder and turmeric, then add chicken pieces. Brown the chicken on all sides before adding tomatoes and coconut milk. Simmer for 20–25 minutes until the chicken is cooked through. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve with rice or naan bread.
